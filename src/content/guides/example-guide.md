Carers Allowance is a benefit for people who spend at least 35 hours a week caring for someone with substantial caring needs. This example guide is here to confirm that the content collection schema is wired up correctly before real guides are added.

## Who might qualify?

You may be eligible if you care for someone receiving certain disability benefits, you earn below the earnings limit, and you are not in full-time education. Rules can change, so always check the latest government guidance alongside any checker tool.

## How BenefitsCheck can help

BenefitsCheck offers free, plain-English checkers to help you understand whether you might qualify for support. Use the related tool linked from this guide to answer a few questions and see tailored next steps.

## What to do next

If the checker suggests you may be eligible, gather details about your caring hours, income, and the person you care for before applying through GOV.UK. Keep records of any correspondence in case you need to follow up later.
